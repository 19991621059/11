About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of unique monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become - but the monsters become more dangerous too!

Choose your character, develop your strategy, and see how long you can last against the ever-increasing monster hordes. Collect experience gems to level up and choose from a variety of weapons and passive items to create powerful synergies.

How To Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character and avoid monsters. Your character automatically attacks nearby enemies with their equipped weapon. Collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up and choose new weapons or upgrades.

As time progresses, the difficulty increases, bringing more challenging monsters and epic boss battles. How long can you survive?
